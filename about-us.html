About Us

EE Wine, Inc., now trading as Wine Energy, employs more than 170 service-conscious individuals and operates a fleet of 30 trucks and vehicles. They sell over 33 million gallons of heating oil and other petroleum products every year and serve nearly 3000 residential and commercial customers throughout Northern Virginia. Wine Energy also operates a Dairy Queen and 11 WI-NOT STOP® convenience stores with 5 café’s offering freshly made pizza, chicken, burgers & fries.

Wine Energy HVAC Division is class A licensed by the state of Virginia to install the latest energy efficient heating, ventilation and air conditioning systems at a competitively low price.

Crystal Clean Duct Services, a Wine Energy company, exists to improve your air quality needs. Air duct cleaning removes airborne contaminates such as bacteria, fungus, mold spores, pet dander & pollen which may help to alleviate allergy & asthma symptoms. Wine Energy is a one-stop-shop for all of your heating and air conditioning needs.

In today’s fast-paced, auto-dependent society, keeping your car in good running order is important. But finding someone who is qualified, trustworthy and affordable to maintain your car can be a major problem. That’s why Wine Energy opened Quik Serv Auto Maintenance Center, conveniently located at 9110 Centreville Road in Manassas.

  • Timeline Event

    EE Wine was started in the early 1960’s by Edward E. Wine with only five accounts given to him by Humble Oil. At the end of the first year, with his wife Marie at the books, they sold an impressive 166,000 gallons of heating oil. In just four years they surpassed the 1 million gallon mark.

    1960

  • Timeline Event

    By 1973 they had incorporated and purchased Humble Oil’s tanks and equipment, and took direct control of local accounts.

    1973

  • Timeline Event

    Edward’s son Donovan became president in 1978 and today the company continues to expand, offering a growing variety of energy related services to Northern Virginia residents.

    1978

  • Timeline Event

    In 1994 EE Wine opened their first gas station and convenience store in Manassas, VA. Today we operate a Dairy Queen and 11 WI-NOT STOP® convenience stores throughout Manassas, Prince William and Fauquier Counties. We operate five WI-NOT STOP® Café’s dedicated to serving a home cooked meal, from our kitchen to you, delivered fast & fresh guaranteed! WI-NOT STOP® satisfies our customers’ fuel needs while providing world-class service and the products they demand.

    1994

  • Timeline Event

    In 2004 EE Wine opened the Bealeton Dairy Queen® with a sit down dining room with food served to your table. You won’t find this type of service in a fast food chain in our area– other than at the Dairy Queen of Bealeton! The interior décor is classic to the 1950’s era and provides a warm and inviting atmosphere to enjoy food hot off the grill, or ice cream for a chill!

    2004

  • Timeline Event

    In 2012 the company decided to rebrand as Wine Energy to reflect our growth beyond a strictly fuel services company. However, success and a new corporate image haven’t spoiled our family-friendly atmosphere. As always, quality products and a tradition of people-focused service remain hallmarks of Wine Energy.

    2012
